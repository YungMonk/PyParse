举报收藏

岗位职责

1. 根据部门总体市场策略编制自己分管的市场的销售计划；通过各种渠道发展新客户包括在现有客户中做深度挖掘；通过开发新客户，稳定老客户，以力争达到所确定的销售收入目标；按时完成销售主管分派的工作；

2. 为了保持产品在市场上的竞争性，随时关注客户和市场的变化，分析原因并向销售经理提出建议与意见；

3. 为了提高客户满意度，需加强对现有客户的维护和管理，协助客服部门保证现有客户的续保。

 

岗位要求

1. 本科学历，1年以上保险销售背景者佳；

2. 对保险市场和展业工作有较深刻认知；

3. 有良好的市场判断能力和开拓能力，有极强的团队合作精神；

4. 熟练操作办公软件；
5. 优秀的英语听. 说. 读. 写能力;较强的观察力和应变能力。

 
Job Responsibilities

1. develop new customers through various channels according to the marketing strategy;, including doing the depth of excavation of existing customers; strive to achieve sales target by developing new customers, stabilize old customers. Complete the work assigned by sales director on time.

2. In order to maintain the competence of our products in the market, you should pay close attention on changing of customer and markets, analyze the reasons and make recommendations and opinions to Sales Manager;
3. In order to improve customer satisfaction, you should strengthen the maintenance and management of existing customers and assist the CS department to ensure renewal of existing customers; 

 

Job Requirements

1. Bachelor degree ,1 year or above experience of insurance sales is a plus;

2. Have profound awareness of insurance market and exhibition work;

3. Good judgment and ability to open up the market with strong team spirit;

4. Familiar with the operation of Microsoft office;

5. Good English speaking ,reading and writing skills; strong ability of observation and resilience.

About MSH CHINA

Founded in 2001, MSH China is a leading high-end health insurance services provider in China. Our headquarters and operations center is in Shanghai. We have branch offices in Beijing and Guangzhou, and service offices in Dalian, Wuhan, Shenzhen, Chengdu and Suzhou. MSH China is the Asia pacific headquarter of MSH INTERNATIONAL. In addition, MSH INTERNATIONAL has a branch office in Singapore to serve the rest of the Asia-Pacific region.

 

MSH China\'s main business is to provide high-end insurance products and far-reaching service to corporate clients and individuals. Currently, we hold the largest market share in China\'s high-end health insurance and serve over 1,000 companies, many of which are Fortune 500 companies.

Brief Introduction of MSH INTERNATIONAL

Founded in 1974, MSH INTERNATIONAL is a world leader in the design and management of international healthcare solutions.  Via its four regional headquarters in Paris, Calgary, Dubai and Shanghai, MSH INTERNATIONAL provides 24/7, round-clock assistance in 40 languages for its 2,000 corporate clients and 330,000 insured members across 194 countries.  Its majority shareholder is the House of Rothschild, a major player in the financial industry in Europe for more than two centuries. It is the majority shareholder of SIACI SAINT HONORE, to which MSH INTERNATIONAL belongs

MSH CHINA 简介
