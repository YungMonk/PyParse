职位信息

职位描述：
Senior Structure/Civil Engineer
Responsibilities:

？ Set up a list of design requirements, drawing and detailed drawings list, in compliance to the Client’s Design Intent by considering economical and technical key datas, intended products and potential savings.
？ Observe the implementation of the stipulated design intent, direct the Local Design Institute (Detailed Design) and the Contractors / Suppliers (Workshop Design) to obey the design intent.
？ Check design content for plausibility (i.e. reinforcement content, dimension of structure) find out design inconsistencies and missing design details, if more information needed notify superiors to clarify with Client.
？ Prepare Reference Bill of Quantities, Drawing lists and Technical Specifications, Room Books and Finishing Schedules, Door lists.
？ Organize and guide technical clarifications during the tendering phase, document and file objective Analytical Price Evaluation as a basis of the Final Contract Negotiation Meeting under the supervision of the CM / PM.
？ Site Supervision (Quality, Safety, Progress) .
？ Observe quality and trigger corrective actions in case of non-compliance by the provided forms proactively, initiate / follow up / close out Quality Non Conformance.

Requirement：
？ Civil Engineer; Bachelor or Master Degree
？ 5 years working experience in Civil Engineering
？ Fluent English both oral and written

职能类别： 结构/土木/土建工程师
